\section{Further Promising Directions}
\label{sec:future}

Despite notable successes reviewed up to this point, we believe much value remains yet to be recruited from bioscience literature.
Among highlighted possibilities, particularly low-hanging fruit appears to remain on account of scarce interdisciplinary familiarity.

Here, we suggest several emerging directions where EC stands to benefit from bioscience inspiration.
These possibilities, of course, are by no means exhaustive --- and we anticipate new directions will continue to unfold far into the future as interdisciplinary conversations and both fields continue to develop.
